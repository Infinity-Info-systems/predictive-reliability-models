# Operational Intelligence

## Purpose

Operational intelligence turns signals into decision support by combining telemetry, context, and response guidance.
It helps the team decide what the signal means, what action it should trigger, and how to measure whether that action helped.

## Intelligence Areas

- signal correlation
- incident context
- service impact
- confidence scoring
- response recommendations
- context enrichment
- decision traceability

## Review Questions

- can the team explain why an alert fired?
- does the signal point to a service outcome?
- can the response be prioritized?
- are model outputs understandable?

## Use

Use this page when the team needs to understand how a score or alert should influence an operational decision.

## Evidence To Collect

- correlation logic
- response guidance
- context mapping
- decision logs

## Practical Value

Operational intelligence should explain why an alert matters and what should happen next.
It should also help operators see whether the signal is strong enough to justify automation or escalation.
