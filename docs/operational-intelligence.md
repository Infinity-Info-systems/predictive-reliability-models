# Operational Intelligence

## Purpose

Operational intelligence turns signals into decision support by combining telemetry, context, and response guidance.

## Intelligence Areas

- signal correlation
- incident context
- service impact
- confidence scoring
- response recommendations
- context enrichment
- decision traceability

## Review Questions

- can the team explain why an alert fired?
- does the signal point to a service outcome?
- can the response be prioritized?
- are model outputs understandable?

## Evidence To Collect

- correlation logic
- response guidance
- context mapping
- decision logs

## Practical Value

Operational intelligence should explain why an alert matters and what should happen next.
