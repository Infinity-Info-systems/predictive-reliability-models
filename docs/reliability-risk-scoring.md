# Reliability Risk Scoring

## Purpose

Reliability risk scoring helps teams rank early warning signals by the likelihood and impact of failure.

## Scoring Dimensions

- likelihood
- impact
- confidence
- recurrence potential
- response urgency
- service criticality

## Example Scale

- low
- medium
- high
- critical

## Use

Use this scoring model to prioritize what deserves human attention first and what can be monitored further.

## How To Use

- score the signal and the consequence
- separate risk from noise
- revisit the score after validation
