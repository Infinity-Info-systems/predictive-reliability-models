# Reliability Risk Model

## Purpose

Describe how signals map to reliability risk and how that risk should be reviewed.

## Risk Areas

- service behavior
- dependency fragility
- capacity pressure
- operational drift
- change risk
- signal confidence

## Use

- rank the risks by impact
- connect risk to response and review

## Use

Use this model to translate predictive signals into a practical reliability risk view that teams can review and act on.
